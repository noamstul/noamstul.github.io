Catering

BY THE BEST IN THE BUSINESS

After honing our craft for over 50 years, a meal catered by CKC is instantly recognizable and simply unforgettable. Our delicious and distinctive dishes delight every palate. Our vibrant salads are plated with pizzazz, like our gorgeous spinach pear pecan salad, our tangy strawberry and mandarin salad, and our luscious pesto pasta salad. Our fish tables will please the pickiest of pescatarians, with gourmet sushi platters, luxurious lox trays, seared ahi tuna skewers, and sumptuous salmon fillets with our legendary dill sauce.

And who can forget some of the delicious main dishes that have put us on the map? Our heavenly stuffed capon, saucy sweet and sour meatballs, tantalizing schnitzel, divine egg rolls, timeless carrot tzimmes, and world famous smoked meat. Don’t forget to save room for dessert because a plethora of pastries await! Decadent chocolate cakes, irresistible pecan pies, rainbow fruit platters, moist coffee cakes, chewy chocolate chip cookies, creamy opera cakes, and more! Let us cater your next event for a culinary experience your guests will adore from beginning to end. No event is too big or too small - weddings, engagement parties, b’nai mitzvah celebrations, bris milahs/baby namings, kiddushes, holiday parties, conferences, corporate meetings, family gatherings, holiday meals - we do it all!

Weddings

There are few celebrations more momentous than your wedding and we’re ready to serve your guests a custom menu that they’ll be reminiscing about for years to come! From the moment they step into the room, they can expect waiters to present them with mouthwatering hors d'oeuvres, from crispy egg rolls to saucey meat skewers. Buffet tables will adorn the halls as far as the eye can see, boasting gourmet sushi platters, seared ahi tuna, delicious lamb chops, and more. After the chuppah, guests can expect a light soup or bright salad. When you tire of the first round of dancing, expect to be served some of the finest cuts of meat, stuffed poultry or sumptuous fillets of fish. And if you still have room for dessert, you can expect to be treated to creamy cakes, elegant mousses, and maybe even our famous strawberries dressed in chocolate tuxedos. At CKC, we have had the privilege of seamlessly catering weddings with well over 1000 guests in attendance!

Bar & Bat Mitzvahs

Their new outfit is adorable, their speech thanking mom and dad is written, and their cousins flew in all the way from Israel. All that’s left to make your child’s B’nai Mitzvah truly spectacular is a meal that Zeidy will be talking about for weeks to come. We’ll provide all the classics, from our extravagant stuffed capon to our renowned smoked meat carving stations. You can also expect saucy meatballs, succulent chicken skewers, extravagant salmon platters, vibrant salads, and dazzling desserts. Let's build a custom menu that your guests will never forget! We can tailor make a menu to the theme of your dreams!!

Corporate/Events

Nobody likes long, boring meetings or awkward office holiday parties...so liven them up with Creative Kosher Catering! Maybe you’re hosting a late night cocktail party, early morning breakfast or a corporate speaking engagement, don’t fret, CKC is there to turn your dream menu into a delicious and unforgettable reality!
