Party Design

NO ONE THROWS A PARTY LIKE DAVID!

Oftentimes the first thing that people will see when they walk in the room is the decor, setting the tone for the festivities to come. At CKC, we have an eye for detail and can provide a glamorous interior design that’s guaranteed to amaze! We can perfectly encapsulate your vision, with colour schemes that project opulence like lavish gold tablecloths with onyx serviettes or bright fuschia tablecloths with pearl white chair coverings. We offer charming centrepieces for every occasion, from candle-lit displays to elegant floral arrangements.

At CKC, we treat every season like it’s spring, with gorgeous flowers tied into most of our designs. Expect to see stunning bouquets of roses, orchids, tulips, lilies, peonies, and hydrangeas. CKC is your one-stop-shop for all things design. Planning a wedding? We can provide a custom ornate chuppah that will be the talk of the congregation! We also provide designer ice sculptures and can hook you up with valet parking, live music, DJs, hair & makeup, photography, videography, and more! Planning a B’nai Mitzvah celebration or chic party? We can work with any theme you have in mind - we’ve seen and done it all! CKC has set up events in every synagogue in Ottawa, the Soloway Jewish Community Centre, the Chateau Laurier, the Lord Elgin Hotel, Minto Suites Hotel, the Sheraton, the Museum of Nature, the Museum of Civilization, and more! If you can dream it, we can do it!
