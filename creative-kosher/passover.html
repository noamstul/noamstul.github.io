NO ONE DOES THE HOLIDAYS LIKE DAVID!

Just hearing the word is enough to put some people into a frenzy. The mere thought of 8 days of cooking is enough to make them wish we had never left Egypt in the first place! But Passover can be both simple and delicious thanks to CKC’s Passover Pickups! Let us treat you to a holiday feast!

It wouldn’t be Pesach without classics like our renowned matzo ball soup, our sweet carrot tzimmes or are scrumptious potato kugel. You can order as much or as little as you want! You can order one dish or eight days of meals to last the whole holiday!
