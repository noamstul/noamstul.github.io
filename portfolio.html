Algonquin College Recreation Centre Redesign

For my midterm this semester, I was tasked with developing a site for the new Algonquin College Recreation Centre. The design was given to my class by the instructor, and we had to recreate it faithfully using only HTML and CSS.

Content Management

Shopify

While we also covered Wordpress and Drupal in this class, the CMS that stuck with me was Shopify. Not only did I find it incredibly user-friendly, but I also enjoyed the freedom that it offers the user, and the ease with which you can create a stylish and completely functional online store. Not only that, but the behind-the-scenes technology is amazing as well. Shopify provides incredible data for its users, and I loved the fact that I could easily take into account shipping, the original cost of the item, the weight of the items, etc., and use all that information to track the profitability of my store.
